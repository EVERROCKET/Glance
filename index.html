Daily rewinds for your internet browsing footprint.

Glance resurfaces internet visits you made that represent your day. Take a glance at how you spend your time.

How it works

Add to Chrome. Start browsing. That’s it! Every day, Glance updates a collection of up to 10 sites based on the duration for which the URLs are active or remain open.

Your privacy is our top priority

  • We never store your personal data; only you have access to this information

  • All functionality is done on your computer locally only, and we don’t know what you read and visit

  • We never collect your personal data and sell it to third parties
