Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 9, 2021

We do not store your data.

All the data is only stored on your computer, and we have absolutely no access to it. Your privacy is our top priority. That's why the application was built this way.

When you browse a site, the information about the visit is collected through APIs provided by Google Chrome. Glance will then process the data and provide results with the extension. All of this is done locally on your computer. We don't have a server database to store your data, nor will your data be transmitted to elsewhere outside your computer.

The only exception is that we use Google Analytics to track our product usage. We track things like session duration and time of session to learn how users engage with the product. Although Google Analytics plants a permanent cookie on your web browser to identify you as a unique user the next time you visit this site, the cookie cannot be used by anyone but Google. Google’s ability to use and share information collected by Google Analytics about your visits to this site is restricted by the Google Analytics Terms of Use and the Google Pricacy Policy. You can prevent Google Analytics from recognizing you on return visits to this site by disabling cookies on your browser.

If you have any questions, please contact us at contact@glance.today.
