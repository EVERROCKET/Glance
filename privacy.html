Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 11, 2020

We do not store your data.

All the data is only stored on your computer, and we have absolutely no access to it. Your privacy is our top priority. That's why the application was built this way.

When you browse a site, the information about the visit is collected through APIs provided by Google Chrome. Glance will then process the data and provide results with the extension. All of this is done locally on your computer. We don't have a server database to store your data, nor will your data be transferred to elsewhere outside your computer.
